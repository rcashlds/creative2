About the Artist

Maddie hails from Idaho but currently lives in Utah while pursuing a BFA in Illustration from Brigham Young University. She served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Oklahoma. She also has three younger siblings and a loving family who have encouraged her to pursue her passion for art since a young age.

Her work has been exhibited in the Springville Art Museum, in the BYU Harris Fine Arts Center, and on utility boxes in the Meridian area of Idaho.
